about us

.........the springboard of intellectual greatness

conception of grace academy

grace academy was born out of the love and passion of mr chigozie onwugbenu for education in memory
and honour of his beloved mother, mrs grace onwugbenu, who was a passionate advocate of good child up bringing and hardwork.
as can be easily noticed today, not all parents are parent with the mind set for transformative and responsible up bringing of children.
a peep into the structure and manner of trending parenting, you will discover that majority of parent are devoid of the fear of god in the early child up bringing. this is a major contributor to faculty leadership in all the sectors of the economy in the nation. mrs grace onwugbenu believed that to change the future, we must begin early by shaping and changing the early years of our children at home and school respectively. this philosophy means, if you want tommorow to be good, you must impact positively on today's child who will in turn, effect positive change tomorrow. that is the motivation that powered the conception of grace academy.

the philosophy of grace academy

grace academy is a school committed to an all round qualitative education with emphasis on academic and moral excellence. the school is making serious arrangement to invest massively in modern learning technology and has assembled competent and committed teaching and administrative personnel, as well as a conducive environment. we are committed to providing the highest academic standards, as well as enriching lives through spiritual development. small class size concept is adopted with it's main benefits, including individualized attention and care in addition to teacher accessibility. the aim is to produce critical thinking and god minded children who will positively, impact the larger society. grace academy operates a curricular programme which focuses on congintive, affective and psychomotor domains of the learners.

aims and objectives

since the society is becoming complex by the day, the education of children of our nation calls for a dynamic approach to arrest the ugly trend of "get-rich-quick" syndrone and give the children the kind of education that makes for a sound, spiritual, moral, educational and total development of the children. this is the only way to guarantee a sound future and hope for the nation.
