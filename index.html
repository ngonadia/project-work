about our school

grace academy nnewichi, founded in september 2024, is the brain-child of the proprietor, mr chigozie onwugbenu (tokumbo),
whose passion for education, godliness and moral excellence has been the driving force for this major investment in the education industry.
in a world where our youths are growing up to be plagued with the "get-rich-quick" syndrone, and academic and moral absolutes are snubbed, mr chigozie
onwugbenu is committed to helping discover their purpose and moral divection in life. he is particularly concerned about grooming
a new generation of future leaders with deep convictions and sound values that are rooted in god's word and hardwork.
